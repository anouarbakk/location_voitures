This project, developed for our university course "Systems and Data Integration," is the result of our efforts to create a car renting website that unifies various systems and data sources into a single, user-friendly platform. Our goal is to simplify the car rental process by integrating features like real-time vehicle availability, booking, customer management, and payment processing. This integration will streamline operations, reduce redundancies, and ensure data consistency, ultimately improving the experience for both customers and administrators. Through this website, users can effortlessly browse, book, and manage their rentals, while the backend efficiently handles data flow between different components, enhancing functionality and reliability.
